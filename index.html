'Sada Siva Samarambham
Sankaracharya madhyamam
Asmadacharya paryantam
Vande Guru parambaram'

'सदाशिव समारम्भाम्
शंकराचार्य मध्यमाम्
अस्मद् आचार्य पर्यन्ताम्
वंदे गुरु परम्पराम्'

'I Salute Lord Siva, Sri Veda Vyasa Maharshi, Sri Adi Sankaracharya and my Guru Pujya Swami Dayananda Saraswathiji'

Swami Turya Chaitanya is a disciple of Swami Dayananada Saraswathi of Arsha Vidya Gurukulam, a famous traditional teacher of Vedanta. Swamiji has been teaching Vedanta and Sanskrit for the past 25 years in and around Kerala, particularly at Cochin, where his Ashram is located. He holds a Masters Degree in Sociology. Hundreds of spiritual aspirants have benefited by the inspiring talks of Swami, and are capable of seeing the correct meanings of ambiguous phrases of Vedic texts, especially the Vedanta portion called Upanishads. His teaching is simple and a person who is not exposed to the spiritual side of thinking will get an opening in to the profound vision of ancient Vedic sages.
