न त्वेवाहं जातु नासं न त्वं नेमे जनाधिपा: |
न चैव न भविष्याम: सर्वे वयमत: परम् || 12||
na tvevāhaṁ jātu nāsaṁ na tvaṁ neme janādhipāh na chaiva na bhaviṣhyāmaḥ sarve vayamataḥ param'

There was not a time that I did not exist, nor you nor these kings. Nor will any of us cease to exist in the future'. (Gita 2.12).
H.H.Swami Dayananda Saraswathi
